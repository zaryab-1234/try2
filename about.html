About Us

Founded in 1950, Greenhill School is one of the premier Pre-K – Grade 12 independent, co-educational, nonsectarian schools in the nation, preparing students to lead authentic, purposeful lives.

The School’s rich history is defined by continual growth and achievement in a balanced program of academics, arts, athletics, and design and innovation, which together prepare our students for the world ahead. Within a diverse and inclusive community, Greenhill is committed to the core values of excellence, integrity, compassion, and courage. The School’s challenging educational program incorporates experience-based learning and uses leading teaching practices, ensuring a strong academic foundation and inspiring our students to excel in new ways.
